Lab: CloudWatch CPU Alarm

Create an alarm that triggers when EC2 CPU > 70% for 5 minutes.

Step 1: Put Metric Alarm

Adjust thresholds and periods as needed.

aws cloudwatch put-metric-alarm --alarm-name HighCPU --metric-name CPUUtilization --namespace AWS/EC2 --statistic Average --period 300 --threshold 70 --comparison-operator GreaterThanThreshold --dimensions Name=InstanceId,Value= --evaluation-periods 1 --alarm-actions
Route alarms to an SNS topic that emails you.
